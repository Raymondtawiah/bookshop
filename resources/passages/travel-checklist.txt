# Travel Essentials Checklist

---

Use this comprehensive checklist to ensure you have everything you need for your trip.

---

## Documents

* Passport (valid for 6+ months)
* Visa (if required)
* Travel insurance documents
* Flight tickets / booking confirmation
* Hotel reservation / accommodation details
* Emergency contact information
* Copies of important documents (kept separately)

---

## Money & Finance

* Local currency / cash
* Credit/debit cards
* Travel money card
* Bank statement (for visa purposes)
* Emergency fund

---

## Electronics

* Phone + charger
* Power bank
* Universal adapter
* Camera
* Laptop/tablet (if needed)

---

## Clothing

* Underwear
* Socks
* T-shirts
* Pants/shorts
* Jacket/sweater
* Sleepwear
* Comfortable shoes
* Formal attire (if needed)

---

## Toiletries

* Toothbrush + toothpaste
* Shampoo + conditioner
* Soap/body wash
* Deodorant
* Sunscreen
* Medications
* First aid kit

---

## Work & Business

* Business cards
* Laptop + charger
* Notepad + pen
* Presentation materials

---

## Entertainment

* Books/e-reader
* Headphones
* Games
* Downloaded movies/music

---

## Safety

* Travel insurance
* Emergency contacts
* Local embassy information
* Emergency numbers

---

## Health

* Pres medications
* First aid supplies
* Hand sanitizer
* Face masks
* Thermometer

---

**Remember to check weather forecast before packing!**
